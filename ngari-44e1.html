阿里暗夜公园 · 44E1

Ngari Dark Sky Park

阿里地区 噶尔县 · 西藏，中国 · 2021.06.22 · 02:19
Gar County · Ngari Prefecture · Tibet, China · 2021.06.22 · 02:19

深夜两点十九分，银河从黑暗深处沉下来。风声轻得几乎听不见，星光没有闪烁，只是在静静燃烧。这是高原最诚实的时刻——没有光害，没有声音，没有时间。

At 2:19 in the morning, the Milky Way sinks out of the dark. The wind is so soft it is hardly sound, and the stars do not twinkle; they simply burn in place. It is the plateau at its most honest—no light pollution, no noise, and for a while, almost no sense of time.
