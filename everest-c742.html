珠峰大本营 · C742

Everest North Base Camp

日喀则市 定日县 · 西藏，中国 · 2021.06.11 · 18:58
Tingri County · Shigatse · Tibet, China · 2021.06.11 · 18:58

在日落抵达珠峰北坡的那一刻，山体被金色轻触，光线像从远古落下。空气稀薄，世界安静，每一秒都像被海拔重新定义。

Arriving at the north side of Everest at sunset, the summit is touched by a thin layer of gold, as if the light had fallen from another age. The air is thin, the world is quiet, and every second feels redefined by altitude.
