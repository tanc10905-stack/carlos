Guge Kingdom Ruins · Cave Complex

阿里地区 札达县 · 西藏，中国 · 2021.06.21 · 16:53
Zhada County · Ngari Prefecture · Tibet, China · 2021.06.21 · 16:53

傍晚的风穿过洞穴遗迹。砂土的纹理像时间留下的呼吸，文明的轮廓在光线里变得柔软。这里没有废墟的悲伤，只有大地缓慢而耐心的力量。

Evening wind moves through the cave ruins, and the texture of the sand and earth feels like the breath time has left behind. The outline of a vanished civilization softens in the light. There is no tragedy of ruins here—only the slow, patient strength of the land.
