GUGE KINGDOM RUINS

阿里地区 札达县 · 西藏，中国 · 2021.06.21 · 16:53
Zhada County · Ngari Prefecture · Tibet, China · 2021.06.21 · 16:53

傍晚的风穿过洞穴遗迹。砂土的纹理像时间留下的呼吸，文明的轮廓在光线里变得柔软。这里没有废墟的悲伤，只有大地缓慢而耐心的力量。

In the evening wind passing through the cave ruins, the texture of the sand felt like the breath of time. The contours of a lost civilization softened in the light. There was no sorrow of ruins — only the slow and patient force of the earth.
