About Carlos

关于我

I am a travel storyteller who seeks the tension between nature and design. Each expedition is not only a journey across land but also through inner space. From Tibet to Africa, I explore how silence and form shape emotion.

我是一名旅行叙事者，寻找自然与设计之间的张力。每一次出发，不只是地理的移动，更是内心的行走。从西藏到非洲，我记录沉默与形态如何重塑情感。
