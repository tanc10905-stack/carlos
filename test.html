ABOUT ME
I am a luxury travel content storyteller and brand strategist focused on the China market – and also a genuine high-frequency luxury hotel guest and outdoor traveler. I have experienced high-altitude roads in Tibet and Western Sichuan, mountain regions around Chongqing, and fully offline days in the Mulu National Park rainforest in Sarawak, Malaysia. These journeys give me an intuitive understanding of how Chinese travelers balance nature, safety, comfort, and a sense of freedom.

Since 2019, my work in e-commerce and content has centered on brand narrative, user insight, and conversion paths, rather than chasing vanity metrics. This allows me to design content systems for camps and hotels that connect brand story, user emotion, and measurable results – especially on Chinese social platforms such as Xiaohongshu.
